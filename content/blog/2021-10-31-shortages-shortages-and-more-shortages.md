<p>&nbsp;This is the first time in my 52 years alive that I can remember everything and anything you want being on shortage. I am not just talking the latest tech, but yea I haven't been able to buy a new computer as there are shortages of components and the prices are way out of whack. I am talking food shortages and what not. I see on forums people all over the Country saying no shortages here and there. But, I can tell you where I live I see it and the shelves are bare and what you do find you buy it and it turns out that it might be bad. For example a dozen eggs where bought and we cooked half of the dozen for 3 people and we all bit into the eggs and they were off tasting and bad. By the way on that batch it say large eggs and they were almost as small as Whoopers (not kidding should have taken a picture, I joke the USDA inspector must have been off that day). The next batch bought at a different store looked like large eggs and tasted fine.&nbsp;</p><p>I have been in Publix quite a few times and seen either completely empty shelves or sparsely populated shelves. I hope this doesn't continued but I fear it is here for other year with or without the pandemic continuing. Which quite frankly I think most people don't care anymore and are acting like it doesn't and I feel that other people feel it is over. While I will say I am fully vaccinated so I am just not worrying about dying. I don't want to catch it but I can't control other people. <br /></p>
