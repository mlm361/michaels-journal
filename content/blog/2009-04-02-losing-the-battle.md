<p>Well, today was a bad day for me. Not at school that was actually a great day. But, the day turned south when I went to the doctors office for my 3 month visit. First, I am losing my battle with losing weight. I have gained 2 pounds. Secondly, my blood pressure has been borderline for years. Well today my doctor put me on blood pressure medication, Benicar. I am taking Benicar for the blood pressure which is slightly elevated, Metformin to keep me from becoming a diabetic. My glucose is elevated but not considered to be diabetic and my triglycerides are high. So, I am staying on my Tricor. He says those 3 could be stopped if I lost a lot of weight. </p>
