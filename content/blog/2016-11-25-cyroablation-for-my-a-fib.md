I had Cyroablation for my A-Fib after having another hospitalization in September of this year. I was at the end of the road for medicine. I had a top rated Cardio surgeon. The actual operation on my heart was a success. My heart is giving me no problem. I did wake up and it felt like it was on fire. However, that quickly went away. The anesthesia I had and tracheal intubation was a different matter. Ever since I woke up I am having a lot of phgleam, extreme sore throat and coughing up bright red blood in the phgleam. I have been intubated before and have never had a sore throat like this. This is constant sore throat. Well, lets hope tomorrow brings relief.
