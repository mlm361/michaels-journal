### _**From Peacock:**_

* 🎬 **The Day of the Jackal** – _Season 1, Episodes 4–10_ ⭐⭐⭐⭐
  **No spoilers!**

I finished the first season, and all I have to say is: _Bravo._
There was action, mystery, intrigue—and more action. Some parts felt wildly exaggerated; I can’t imagine all of it happening outside the realm of TV drama. But other parts? Absolutely plausible—though more likely orchestrated by nation-states than rogue, non-state actors.

I hope they make a second season—there's definitely room for it. Maybe even a third. But no more than that.

As I said in a previous post, when it comes to TV, the British really know what they’re doing. 🎬

### _**From Prime Video:**_

* 🎬 **The Chosen** – _Season 5, Episodes 2–5_ ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
  **“No spoilers” doesn’t quite apply here—it’s based on the Bible.**

Not much to say other than: _Wow._
It’s been a great season so far—and with such incredible source material, how could it not be? 😊
Fantastic true story, outstanding acting.
**Best series EVER.** 🎬
