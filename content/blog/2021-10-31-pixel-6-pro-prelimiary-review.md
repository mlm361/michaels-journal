I do say the one biggest hardware flaw I see with the phone is the in display fingerprint sensor. It is awful performance. I wished Google would have stuck with the rear fingerprint sensor it was awesome had some hidden features like pulling down the notification area and was just in the right place for your finger. <br /></p><p>Haven't tried the the camera's yet so can't say about the quality and will update soon as I do.
