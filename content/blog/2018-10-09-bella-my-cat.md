Bella, my cat was hit by a car this morning sometime around 5AM to 6AM. She died instantly. I will miss her so much and already miss her. I remember the day she just showed up to our house. She picked me to love like she did. I loved her so much. She had 2 litters of kittens while she was here. I got Katie out of the second litter and she was my favorite like her mother and they both did not want to stay in the house and yard. Both were taken by cars from me. I think I am done having animals. <br>
<br>
Trooper my very first dog I had to give away because of my first job. <br>
Henry my second dog died of old age after having glaucoma and food allergies, but it was old age that got him. <br>
Taco my third dog died of seizures that started all of the sudden one day. I got him from my sister who someone rescued from abuse. He loved me and me him just like Henry. <br>
Then Katie by a car out on a busy street, now Bella. I don’t think I can take much more heart break.<br>
I will miss you Bella you loved me for 5 years and gave me a lot of memories.
