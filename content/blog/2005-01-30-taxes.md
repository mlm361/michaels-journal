Lets go to a flat tax so everyone pays the same no matter if they make $1 or 1 million. Also, I getting tired of cable, satellite, cell phones companies and telephone companies raising their rates with these so called fees and taxes. They aren't Government Taxes they are ways to collect more money from us.
