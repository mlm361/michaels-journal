<p><br />
I’ve never been more productive with computers than I am right now. I have several projects in progress, and all of it has been made possible with the help of AI.</p>
<p>I know AI is a hot-button issue depending on who you talk to—ranging from environmental concerns to broader ethical debates. But for me, it has made a real difference. It’s helped me get the most out of my technology, and honestly, if this had existed 10 or 15 years ago, I might have ended up in a completely different career.</p>
<p>So, what has it helped me with? Well, I’ve tried things I never would have attempted before. For example, I was never quite sure what PowerShell was or how to use it. Now, I just describe what I want to do, and the AI generates the scripts or code for me. Not only does it make me more successful and faster, but I’m also learning as I go—because it explains what it’s doing, why it’s doing it, and how it all works.</p>
<p>There are other examples too, but overall, I’m hopeful. I’d love to see AI evolve into something like the computers on <em>Star Trek</em>, or to a lesser extent, <em>Star Wars</em>—always in the background, helping when asked.</p>
