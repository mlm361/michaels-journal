My cell phone hinge broke on Monday. Tuesday, I went into the Sprint PCS store and I thought I would be in for a great debate and verbal argument over my phone. Nope, I was pleasantly surprise. They ordered me a new phone since it was under manufacture's warranty and I added the total protection in case my replacement phone broke for any reason. So far I have had a great experience with Sprint PCS for wireless. I just got an advertisement for their new EVDO card for laptop access. I am weighing whether or not I would sign up. I am not quite sure yet. I just wish they would add a tower or two in Paris, Tennessee. Then I will be all set.<br />But, on the same token since GTE became Verizon. I have had great service with them especially their Fios Internet Service. I have had an occasional hiccup with this or that with Verizon. But, overall, not bad with all their services I subscribe to. <br />Work has been rough lately. They changed my shift so that I can help train new hires. I got a slight bump in pay that is always good. Hope everyone else is doing great. Please keep my cousins, uncles and aunts in Connecticut in your prayers. They are going through some rough times.
