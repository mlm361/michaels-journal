Well, I was supposed to work all weekend. That didn't happen. I went to the emergency room Thursday night and was admitted to the hospital. I finally got released on Saturday Afternoon. I even passed out in the emergency room and bit my lip. I don't remember passing out. But, I do remember the lip because it hurt when I woke up. The diagnosis is unclear. I believe it was either food poisoning or diverticulitis. <br />The emergency room personnel, according to my dad, were treating me like it was a heart attack. I knew it was not that. All because I said my chest hurt. My chest hurt because I had be vomiting for an hour before going to the emergency room. So, the final outcome: I feel better and looking forward to going back to work. I have 3 doctors appointment next week for follow up. Oh and by the way no heart attack that was ruled out almost immediately.
