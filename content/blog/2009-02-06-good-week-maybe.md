<p>Well, as far as school goes it was a great week. I kicked butt on my tests. However, for the first time in my left I got a stye in my right eye. It hurts and itches like the chickenpox. </p>  <p>Praise the Lord he gave me an excellent Ophthalmologist. She had her nurse return my call and had an instruction sheet for a salt water warm compress and a sample of an eye antibiotic ointment. I picked it up the same day. Thanks Dr. Anglin. My eye still itches however, I think the stye is starting to resolve itself my lower eyelid is only 1/4 of its size since the beginning of the week. If I never get one of these again, I will consider it a miracle. This was my first time having one and I did not like the experience. </p>
