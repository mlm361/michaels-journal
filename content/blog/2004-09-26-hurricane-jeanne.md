Well, we are experiencing some of the worse that Jeanne has to offer. We still have power but we do have minor roof damage and the winds are continuing to pick up. Power has gone off and on several times. We have a lot of trees down too. I got off work early and have to go in again tonight at 6:00 PM. I will try to update you as much as possible. My mom and niece went to my brother's house and they have lost power. During Hurricane Frances they kept power the whole time. This time we have so far. Well, going to try and get some more sleep if these hiccups stop.
