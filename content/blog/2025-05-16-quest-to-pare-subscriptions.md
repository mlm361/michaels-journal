<p>In my continuing battle with subscription fatigue, I’ve decided to drop
my SiriusXM satellite radio service — they wouldn’t match the rate I had
before, so I let it go 👋. For two years, I paid just $6.98 total (taxes
included). It wasn’t the top-tier plan, but it suited my needs,
especially since I used it two to four times a week. That subscription
ends next month.</p>
<p>I’m also parting ways with my free Netflix access, which came bundled
with a Verizon Peacock deal last year. That runs out in July, and
honestly, I’m not sure I’ll pay for Netflix on its own. Lately, I’ve
been watching more on Prime Video, Disney+, and my own Plex server
anyway 🎬.</p>
