<p>My mother told me today that my cousin, Cindy Shaw Rounds, passed away
in her sleep in Jacksonville at the age of 63, either last night or this
morning. I will miss her greatly. I often saw and spoke with her at
funerals for my great-aunts, uncles, grandparents, and at family
reunions. I also have fond memories of staying with her and my other
cousins in Jacksonville for about two weeks when I was a preteen while
my dad was working there. I will cherish the memories I had of her. </p>
