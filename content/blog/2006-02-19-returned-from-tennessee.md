Well, I have returned from Tennessee. I will posting some pictures on next months website update. I did not find any land to purchase and I would recommend not going to Greeneville, Tennessee. It was a dirty city and area. The background of the mountains was the only good point. <br />While on this trip I went to Asheville, NC to look at a Deltec home. They are round homes and stand up to Hurricanes and Tornados quite well. I was very impressed at the model home and the factory where the components are made. <br />I enjoyed Asheville, NC except the road system which was very confusing. I went to the Biltmore estate while in Asheville and liked the house alot. <br />Well, that should be my last extended time off for a while. I want to build up my time.
