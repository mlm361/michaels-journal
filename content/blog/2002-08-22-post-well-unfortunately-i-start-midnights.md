Well, unfortunately I start midnights tonight. For the next 28 days my life will come to a busy zombie movie. Work, sleep, eat, doing employment things for a new perspective job and more work. But, I have a good concept that I wish I could market. A PC that is running some operating system (Linux, Windows XP Professional, etc...) with multiple processors. This would be the house server. Every house would have one. Every thing in the house would have a Ethernet connection. The house server would store all your DVD's and CD's. Your TV Signal would pass through it. Your Internet connection would be tied into it. Your PC could pass on that episode of The Shield or Smallville that it recorded as the DVR to any TV you are watching in any room. While your refrigerator is telling the house server that the ice machine is filling up and someone requests a CD be played in the bathroom while they take a shower. That is the next thing. Of course you would have to have another computer that is hooked up to the house server so that you play games. The house server would also be the answering machine, fax machine and etc......
