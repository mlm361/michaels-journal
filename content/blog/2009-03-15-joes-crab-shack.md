<p>My Cousin Tina and her husband took my Mom and me to Joe's Crab Shack. The food was excellent. I recommend the place for the food. I had the crab of course. They make an awesome Lobster Bisque soup. </p>  <p>However, we had to wait an hour and half to get a table and the service was not the greatest. That was the only negatives. </p>
